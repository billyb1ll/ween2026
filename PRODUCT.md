# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Freshmen (New Students):** Entering university life, seeking clear orientation information, feeling a mix of excitement and anxiety. They need a welcoming, clear, and reassuring path to get oriented.
- **Seniors (Organizers/Pro):** Managing orientation events, sharing memories, hyping up the house, and coordinating activities. They need efficient tools to post updates, share content, and run interactive features.

## Product Purpose
- A unified main portal that consolidates all freshman orientation features (Freshmen Portal, Pro Portal, Memory Board, Hype Board, Vibe Check, etc.) for **Baan 7**.
- Minimizes orientation anxiety by presenting information clearly, while creating strong community engagement through interactive, real-time features.

## Brand Personality
- **Modern, Warm, and Interactive:** Balances academic prestige with welcoming community warmth.
- **Tone:** Reassuring, exciting, premium, and approachable.
- **Core Goal:** Evoke a sense of new beginnings and belonging, avoiding the clinical feel of a standard web application.

## Anti-references
- **Generic SaaS/Corporate Templates:** No uninspired 50/50 splits, left-content/right-image layouts, or cyan-and-blue palettes.
- **Overused AI Tells:** No bento grids, glassmorphism as a default decoration, mesh gradients, or tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows.
- **Dull Interfaces:** Avoid flat, low-contrast document-like layouts that fail to evoke energy.

## Design Principles
1. **Reduce Friction, Ignite Excitement:** Maintain absolute clarity for administrative tasks (onboarding, schedule) while injecting delightful motion and tactile feedback for engagement.
2. **Tactile Storytelling:** Anchor the user journey visually (e.g., via the connecting SVG line or overlapping layers) to make the digital experience feel like a physical, guided tour.
3. **Unconventional Cadence:** Avoid repetitive card grids and layouts. Break grids intentionally, vary spacing, and stagger elements to establish a premium, custom rhythm.
4. **Cohesive Diversity:** Present different portals/dashboards (Memory Board, Hype Board, Vibe Check) as unified components of the Baan 7 ecosystem, sharing an Ivory-based, high-contrast visual DNA.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Target:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- **Color Contrast:** Text must meet a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 against its background.
- **Adaptability:** Responsive, mobile-first design as many freshmen access the portal on their phones during orientation.
- **Motion:** Respect user preference for reduced motion (`prefers-reduced-motion`) with instant transitions or simple crossfades.
